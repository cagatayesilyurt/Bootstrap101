  • 18 March Martyrs' Day Administrative Leave Announcement

    In accordance with the Circular published in the Official Gazette dated 15 October 2005 and numbered 25967, the widows and orphans of martyrs and their mothers, fathers and siblings working at our University will be considered on administrative leave on 18 March Martyrs' Day.

  • Martial Arts Course Announcement

    A Martial Arts Course for women is planned by our University's Campus Recreation Office. The course will consist of different sessions in weekly periods starting from 04.03.2024. Participants will be able to choose one of the sessions specified in the Google Form Document prepared for application.

  • Attention to Our Students: ESTÜ-TEKNOTAM Academy Begins

    ESTU-TEKNOTAM ACADEMY, which was established with the cooperation protocol signed between EKNOTAM Technology Research and Development Center Industry and Trade Limited Company and our University, is starting its new era! Don't miss the opportunity to attend the trainings of ESTÜ-TEKNOTAM Academy, where you will receive training from industry experts in the 2023-2024 Academic Year Spring Semester!

  • International Exchange Programs Language Exam Announcement

    The first phase of the International Exchange Programs Language Exam, which our students and staff who want to benefit from the Erasmus program can benefit from, will be held on March 9, 2024, and the second phase will be held on March 16-17, 2024. Applications will be received at basvuru.eskisehir.edu.tr between 23 February 2024 - 5 March 2024. For details, visit uib.eskisehir.edu.tr.
